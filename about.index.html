Let's know about the famous car companies of the world



1. Volkswagen

Revenue: 302 billion dollars

The Volkswagen Group is one of the largest car manufacturers in the world and its headquarters is located in Wolfsburg, Germany. This group consists of two general divisions: the automotive division and the financial services division. Passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and power engineering business areas are active in the automotive sector. This group is known for owning big automobile names such as Volkswagen, Audi, Seat, Skoda, etc. passenger cars.

2. Toyota Motors

Revenue: 245 billion dollars

The second place in the ranking of the world's automobile companies belongs to Toyota. Toyota Motors is the largest car manufacturer in the world. Toyota is a Japanese multinational company that has a bright and successful presence in the world markets. The headquarters of this company is located in Aichi, Japan. Toyota can be considered one of the largest global companies in terms of revenue. This company continues its production activities through 50 manufacturing plants in 30 countries.

3. Daimler

Revenue: 174 billion dollars

Daimler AG is one of the largest manufacturers and suppliers of high-quality commercial vehicles worldwide. Daimler headquarters is located in Stuttgart, Germany. This company has a strong presence in the global car market through Benz passenger cars, Daimler trucks, all kinds of vans, buses and financial services.

4. Ford

Revenue: 135 billion dollars

It is impossible to talk about the ranking of automobile companies in the world without mentioning the name of Ford. Ford Motor Company is an American multinational automobile manufacturer that was founded in 1919 in Delaware, USA. With approximately 200,000 employees worldwide, Ford operates and serves customers with a full line of passenger cars, trucks, electric vehicles, and more. Among other Ford brands, we can mention Lincoln.

5. BMW Group

Revenue: $128 billion

BMW, or the Bavarian Automobile Group (Bayern), is one of the leading manufacturers of first-class automobiles and motorcycles and is generally one of the top automobile brands in the world. This company started its work in Munich, Germany in 1916 and introduced itself as a truly global company with more than 30 factories and manufacturing and assembly facilities in many countries, producing and selling cars on a global scale.
